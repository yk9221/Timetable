Search/Add Course

1. Searching

  • Enter the course code in the search box to find your desired course

  • When multiple search results appear, select your preferred course from the list of options

  • If only one result is found, it will automatically be added to your list

2. Adding Filters

  • After pressing the "filter" button, you can customize your search further by adding the faculty and session of your choice

  • Default filters include Applied Science & Engineering and Arts & Science for the faculty, along with Fall and Year long courses for the session

3. Excluding Sections

  • If your desired section is full, you have the option to exclude it while generating timetables

4. Clearing all courses

  • Clicking on "Timetable" allows you to clear all the added courses

  • Proceed with caution when using this feature, since it is irreversible

Generate Schedule

1. Navigating

  • Clicking on the arrow buttons or pressing the arrow keys enables navigation to the next or previous timetable

2. Saving

  • Clicking on the save button will add your desired timetable to the saved list, accessible in the "Saved Schedule" tab

Saved Schedule

1. Navigating

  • Clicking on the arrow buttons or pressing the arrow keys enables navigation to the next or previous saved timetable

2. Deleting

  • Clicking on the delete button will remove your timetable from the saved list

Schedule Overview

1. Navigating

  • Clicking on the arrow buttons or pressing the arrow keys enables navigation to the next or previous saved timetable

2. Course Legend

  • The course legend on the left displays the added courses, along with the corresponding colors used in the timetables to represent them

3. Schedule Zoom in

  • Clicking on the desired timetable will provide you with a more detailed view of the timetable

  • By clicking the save button, you can add your preferred schedule to the saved list
